CARS

Cars is a movie about a racer named Lightning McQueen. He's a great racer and he was on his way to winning the Piston Cup. But along the way, his trailer opened and he fell out. He ended up in a town full of other cars who force him to fix the mess he made. McQueen hated the town until he started making friends and found a lover. He ended up leaving the town to race for the Piston Cup. He was losing until he saw his friends supporting him in the pits. He was gonna win but he stopped so that he can help the damaged and injured racer finish the race.
